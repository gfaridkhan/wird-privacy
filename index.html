Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Wird does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Everything stays on your device.

What data do we collect?

None. Wird does not collect any personal information, usage data, location data, or any other data from your device.

Do we use analytics or tracking?

No. Wird contains no analytics tools, no tracking SDKs, and no advertising networks. We have no visibility into how you use the app.

Where is your data stored?

All data generated by Wird — your dhikr counts, goals, history, and settings — is stored locally on your device using Android's private app storage. This data is only accessible to the Wird app and is never transmitted to any server.

Do we share data with third parties?

No. We do not share, sell, rent, or trade any data with any third party. There is no data to share — we do not have it.

Notifications

If you enable reminders, Wird schedules local notifications on your device. These notifications are generated entirely on your device and do not involve any external servers or services.

Children's privacy

Wird is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If we ever change our privacy practices, we will update this page and change the "Last updated" date above. We will never introduce data collection without clearly communicating it.
